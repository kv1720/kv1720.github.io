My life story:

Growing up, I struggled with finding my voice. Being surrounded by self-centered people and distant family did not necessarily make my transition to womanhood easy. I was bullied, nagged at, and pushed to my very last nerve - but I did not let imprudent people antagonize me. I was taught from a very young age that sometimes you need to lose some people in your life to be able to find yourself and fully become indepedent. I live each day building myself to the best person I can be, and being unapologetically me.
